2) the troubles involved with enforcing laws and collecting taxes

What happens if people do not abide by those laws or do not pay tax. It becomes difficult both due to the distances involved and the legal concerns (due to being in separate countries) to have a body to enforce these.

People will mix with people living in their own countries and this creates further complications as they will be closer to the people of their own country than they would be to the other citizens of the new society.

4) logistics of large distances

Large distances and complications with dealing with many different countries, make it difficult to implement infrastructure projects.

This means things like health care, public transportation etc will be difficult to be provided to the citizens of the new society.
